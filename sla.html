    Service Level Agreement (SLA)

    1. Introduction

    ExpHost ("we", "us", "our") provides Minecraft server hosting services with the following guarantees and commitments to our customers ("you", "your").

    2. Uptime Guarantee

    We guarantee a 99% uptime for our Minecraft servers. If we fail to meet this uptime guarantee, you may be eligible for compensation as outlined in Section 7.

    3. Service Provisioning

    Most servers will be up and running within one hour of order confirmation.

    4. Support Availability

    We provide nearly 24/7 support, with a team distributed across various time zones to ensure timely assistance.

    5. Maintenance and Downtime

    Scheduled Maintenance: We will notify you in advance of any scheduled maintenance that may cause downtime.
    Emergency Maintenance: We may need to take down servers or the network for urgent security issues or to assist specific clients.

    6. Data Center Limitations

    We cannot control downtime caused by our data centers and do not guarantee specific resolution times for such incidents.

    7. Security and Data Loss

    We employ top-of-the-line security measures. However, in the event of a breach or data loss, you may be entitled to compensation based on the extent of the loss. Please contact support for assessment and compensation details.

    8. Performance Metrics

    Our services are monitored for performance, and we ensure servers are running optimally. Metrics and logs are available upon request.

    9. Remedies for Breach

    If we fail to meet the SLA terms, you may be eligible for compensation. Compensation details are available upon request and will be assessed based on the impact and duration of the service disruption.

    10. Changes to the SLA

    We reserve the right to modify this SLA. Any changes will be communicated to you in advance.

    11. Contact Information

    For support or questions regarding this SLA, please contact us at support@exphost.net.
